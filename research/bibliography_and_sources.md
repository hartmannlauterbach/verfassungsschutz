## Academic Sources

### Ethnomusicology
1. **Charry, Eric**. "Mande Music: Traditional and Modern Music of the Maninka and Mandinka of Western Africa." University of Chicago Press, 2000.
   - Comprehensive study of Mande music traditions
   - Instrumental analysis
   - Historical development

2. **Skinner, Ryan**. "The Ambivalence of Musical Cosmopolitanism in Bamako." African Music, vol. 8, no. 2, 2009, pp. 9-30.
   - Contemporary Malian music
   - Global influences
   - Cultural identity

3. **Durán, Lucy**. "Mande Music." The Garland Encyclopedia of World Music, vol. 1, 1998.
   - Traditional music analysis
   - Cultural context
   - Instrument studies

### Cultural Studies
1. **Konaté, Massa Makan**. "The Griot's Craft: An Essay on Traditional Storytelling in Mali." Research in African Literatures, vol. 23, no. 2, 1992.
   - Griot tradition analysis
   - Oral history methods
   - Cultural preservation

2. **Cutter, Charles**. "Politics and Music in Mali." Journal of African Cultural Studies, vol. 14, no. 1, 2002.
   - Music and political context
   - Social commentary
   - Cultural policy
