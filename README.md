# Schleini & Mali Musicians Research Project

## Overview
This research project explores and compares two distinct musical traditions:
- **Schleini**: A modern German Techno/Hardtekk electronic music producer
- **Mali Musicians**: Traditional and contemporary musicians from Mali, West Africa

## Research Questions
1. How do these musical traditions differ in their cultural contexts and historical development?
2. What are the key characteristics of each musical style?
3. How do modern and traditional approaches to music production and performance compare?
4. What can we learn from the intersection of electronic and traditional African music?
